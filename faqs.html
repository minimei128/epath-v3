FAQ

What is FAQ?

FAQ stands for Frequently Asked Questions. If you have any questions not answered on this page, feel free to send a message over on Contact Page and we get back to you as soon as we can.

Who can use this website?

The main audience is for Australian high school students however anyone can join if they have interest in helping other students as past students themselves, teachers and people of any interest to high school subjects.

How do become a registered user?

From the homepage, press sign up button to navigate you to register page to fill out your details or hit the login button on the top navigation bar to then direct you to register page from there. There will be an email confirmation when registering to confirm you are the user.

Who do I contact if there is an issue with my account or site?

From Contact Page, send a message with your enquiry and we get back to you as soon as we can by email.

How do I submit resources to Epath?

You would have to be a registered user. Then navigate to Resources Page and on the top right there is an orange button labelled Add Resources. Fill out the detail, upload your file and hit submit. It should appear on the page instantly.

How do I change my avatar?

From the Dashboard page under Account Settings Page.
